AnxSion Concepts Service Policy

Policy Statement

The customer service is a top priority of AnxSion Concepts. All customers will be treated promptly and respectfully without regard to age, gender, sexual orientation, race, ethnicity, disability, language proficiency, social or economic status. AnxSion Concepts,however, can terminate a project with its discreation if it assesses that the behaviour of the customer is consistently poor or the goal of the customer is diverging too greatly from what was planned originally.



Explanation
a. If the project was initially planned to be a standard website and later it is to be an advanced website, or vice versa, they are free to choose so and the project WILL NOT BE terminated. However the prices shall change.
b. If the project was initially planned to be a standard website cloting store, and later it is to be turned into a automotive website, the project WILL BE terminated.
c. If the project involves minor changes, the project WILL NOT BE terminated.

Technical Services: Rules and Regulations

1. The standard web development sercive is NOT a mere tempelate development on a CMS. The web is made exclusively with codes and will be tailored to meet your specific needs. You will require a web administrator or any other individual with the knowledge of HTML to use the website effectively. The Web is always designed to meet all the web standards.

2. The standard web development is open to up to 5 change requests, every subsequent change request shall incure additional charges.

3. The application development service is open only for Android OS. However, if the application is a Progressive Web Application, then it shall work on all operating system.

4. The first major update and subsequent small updates for a year from the date of release shall be free of cost. The application shall be maintained actively for 3 additional years with a small updates to be done for free, while the major updates to be done with additional charges.

Provided, if the application is a PWA, the applications shall be maintained as long as the lifecyle of the website persists. The update charges however, will apply accordingly.

5. The advanced website service shall be regulated by both standard web service and the application service (relating to the PWA) rules mentioned beforehand (Rule 1 to RUle 4). This service shall be highly dependent upon the needs of the customer.

6. The backend service shall be a software and hardware based service. The price of the service can be waived with the presence of pre-existing hardware or software solution of the customer, as may be applicable. The backend service, in itself, shall not consist of standard web service, application service nor advanced website service.

However, these services can be added seperately with independent charges.


Audio Services: Rules and Regulations

7. The composing service shall be bound with a non-disclosure agreement. The customer shall be granted a lineant access to the work in progress material. However, if the customer is disruptive or demands to access the material too frequently, the material and the work in progress materials shall be restricted to the point that the project can be completed smoothly without any disruption.

However, the customer shall not lose access to the material absolutely and the restriction will be resonable.

8. The Sampeling service shall be regulated by the Composing service rules (Rule 7).

9. The mixing and mastering service too shall be regulated by Rule 7.

10. The live performance service will be executed as planned and shall only be open to changes in case of unforeseen or other emergency situations.

11. The payments made for the performers shall not be refundable, as long as there is no default on the performers.

Provided, in case of unforseen events or emergency situation, the customers shall only be eligible for the refund of 80% of the total amount of fees charged.


Art Services: Rules and Regulations

12. The design and consultaion service may include concept generation or proof of concept design assembly.

13. The all the services in this category shall be regulated with a similar access regulations mentioned in the Rule 7.
